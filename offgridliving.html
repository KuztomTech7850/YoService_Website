Off-Grid Living – $30-$50/hr

What’s Possible:
From weekend overnights to full-time life beyond the power lines, we help you build an off‑grid setup that feels stable, safe, and simple to live with. Power, connectivity, and comfort — tuned to how you actually use your space, not just what’s written on a box.

  • RV, van, and tiny‑home solar systems (design, upgrades, and troubleshooting)
  • Battery banks, inverters, charge controllers, and clean wiring layouts
  • Hybrid setups that blend solar, shore, and generator power
  • Low‑draw networking and device charging for remote work or travel
  • Basic monitoring so you always know what you can safely run

Perfect For:
Off‑grid curious, weekend boondockers, full‑time RVers, and cabin or homestead owners who want independence without turning into their own electrician. Whether you’re parking on BLM land for a few days or settling into a long‑term off‑grid site, YoService helps you plan, install, and maintain a system that fits your budget, your rig, and your real‑world lifestyle.
